Foundation

Year 6-7 Mathematics

Building a rock-solid mathematical foundation while introducing advanced problem-solving techniques for competitions.

Learning Objectives:

  • Core Reinforcement: Strengthen understanding of key curriculum topics including algebra, geometry, and probability.
  • Problem Solving: Develop logical reasoning skills essential for higher-level mathematics.
  • AMC Preparation: Targeted practice with past papers and strategies for the Australian Mathematics Competition.

Target Audience:

Students in Years 6 and 7 looking to excel in school and prepare for national competitions.

Beginner Coding

Python Basics

Your first step into the world of programming. Learn the language of the future from scratch to competition readiness.

Learning Objectives:

  • Syntax & Logic: Master variables, loops, conditionals, and functions in Python.
  • Computational Thinking: Learn how to break down complex problems into solvable code.
  • OUCC Preparation: Specific training modules designed for the Oxford University Computing Challenge.

Prerequisites:

No prior coding experience required. Perfect for absolute beginners.

Advanced

VCE Algorithmics (HESS)

A rigorous, university-level subject for students ready to master the logic behind computer science.

Learning Objectives:

  • Algorithmic Mastery: In-depth study of core data structures and algorithms required for elite competitive programming.
  • VCE Preparation: Gain a head start and practical experience with concepts covered in the VCE Algorithmics curriculum.
  • Capstone Project: Students will conceptualize and develop a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) from their own ideas, honing real-world development and project management skills.

Competition Targets:

Aiming for high-level awards in Bebras, CAT, Coding Challenge (CC), and the Australian Informatics Olympiad (AIO).
